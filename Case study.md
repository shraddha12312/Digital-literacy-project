Online  Fraud and Scams in India

What the Crime Is:

AI Voice Cloning is a sophisticated form of social engineering where attackers use Generative Artificial Intelligence to create a near-perfect replica of a person's voice. This technology is weaponized to impersonate trusted individuals, such as family members or colleagues, to manipulate victims into urgent financial transfers.

How It Typically Happens:

Data Harvesting: Attackers "scrape" short audio clips of the target (the person being impersonated) from public social media videos like Instagram Reels or YouTube.

Voice Cloning: Using AI software, the attacker processes the audio to create a synthetic "voice skin" that can read any text with the target’s specific tone and emotion.

The High-Pressure Call: The fraudster calls a relative (the victim), using the cloned voice to claim an emergency—such as being arrested or involved in an accident.

The Demand: The "family member" begs for immediate funds for bail or medical fees, usually via UPI or wire transfer.

The Transfer: Under extreme emotional stress, the victim sends the money before verifying the story.

Who is Typically Targeted:

According to research on Online Fraud and Scams in India, targets are often the parents or elderly relatives of tech-savvy youth. Attackers exploit the victim's emotional bond and their lack of awareness regarding AI capabilities.

Consequences:

The primary consequence is immediate, non-reversible financial loss. For example, a March 2024 incident in Delhi saw a businessman lose ₹10 lakh in minutes. Beyond money, victims suffer long-term psychological trauma and a profound "loss of trust" in digital communication.
