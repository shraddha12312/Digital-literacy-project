Task 4 - Etiquette Guide
B) Create a Social Media Do's and Dont's checklist

Do's (Best Practices)-
	                                                                       
1. Use Privacy Settings: Regularly review and restrict who can see your posts and personal info.
2. Think Before You Post: Ask yourself if you’d be comfortable with a future employer seeing the content.	
3. Verify Before Sharing: Fact-check news or "viral" campus rumors before reposting them to avoid spreading misinformation.	
4. Give Credit: Always tag or credit the original creator when sharing photos, art, or intellectual property.	
5. Build a Professional Brand: Use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase projects, certifications, and academic wins.
	
Don'ts (Avoid These)-

1. Post Illegal Acts: Never post photos or text depicting underage drinking, drug use, or vandalism.
2. Share Sensitive Info: Avoid posting your home address, phone number, or current exact location (live-tagging).
3. Engage with Trolls: Don't get into heated, disrespectful arguments or "flame wars" in public comment sections.
4. Cyberbully or Harass: Never use social media to target, mock, or exclude peers or faculty members.
5. Over-Post During Class: Avoid constant posting during lectures; it reflects poorly on your academic discipline.
